Say Goodbye to Craigslist and Hello to the new online marketplace, Loxodonta. This is a marketplace for students at the University of Virginia to buy and sell all sorts of items from clothes to textbooks to furniture. Click "Create New Listing" to sell an item or "Listings" to start browsing!

Guidelines

Here are some posting guidelines to help you get started

  • Be sure to take a high quality picture of your item
  • Add a detailed description with alternate contact info. We'll provide your student email but feel free to enter another point of contact
  • Pick a category and an accurate condition of your item
  • You're ready to start selling!

Who Are We?

Originally Team E, since we aren't exactly a creative lot, we choose an animal that started with the letter E as our team name. Somehow though, "Elephants" just seemed bogus and boring. So to pretend we're interesting people, we chose Loxodonta, the Latin word for elephant.
