    Who Are We?

    Originally Team E, since we aren't exactly a creative lot, we choose an animal that started with the letter E as our team name. Somehow though, "Elephants" just seemed bogus and boring. So to pretend we're interesting people, we chose Loxodonta, the Latin word for elephant.
